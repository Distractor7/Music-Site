About

My name is Jordan Bendon, I am 25 years old and I've been deeply interested in music since my father introduced me to The Rolling Stones when I was a young boy.

This website is a platform in which I showcase my passion of producing, mixing, collecting and sharing music. This website is a collection of my own creations as well as noteworthy works of others.

I will also explain my own approach to the creative process of producing music.
